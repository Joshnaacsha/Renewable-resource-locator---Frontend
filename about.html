About VORTEX - Our Story

Vortex was born from a shared passion for sustainability and a vision to revolutionize how companies approach renewable energy. As entrepreneurs, we, a group of five pre-final year CSE students— Gibson, Mithra Y, Joshna Acsha S, Prigangshu Chakraborty, and Ricky Davidson—embarked on this journey with a groundbreaking idea. Our mission is to connect companies with optimal locations for renewable energy projects, providing solutions that are not only beneficial but also sustainable for the environment.


Our Mission

Our mission is to empower businesses to make environmentally conscious choices while maximizing their return on investment. We believe in harnessing the power of data science to guide companies in locating and implementing renewable energy solutions efficiently.


Benefits

By leveraging Vortex, companies not only contribute to a greener future but also ensure the sustainability of their operations. Our platform assists in identifying the most suitable locations for solar farms, windmills, hydropower stations, and more, allowing businesses to make informed and sustainable decisions.
